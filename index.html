Biography

Gurli O.K. is a composer, writer and producer with formal training in songwriting and music production. She holds an MA in Creative Music Production, ICMP, London, 2025, and a BA in Songwriting, Det Jyske Musikkonservatorium, 2020. Her work spans composition, production, and release execution across EP and album formats.

Her professional record includes booking agreements with Beatbox Booking, 2017-2019, and Bullet Booking, 2019-2022, alongside recording and collaboration contracts with LACOBR, 2016-2018, and Sony, 2018-2020. These engagements sit within a sustained period of releases supported by live activity and radio support.

Her release catalogue includes the album I Could Be Blossoming Instead, 2021, and EP releases including Metanoia, 2019, Sony, as well as Lunacy, LACOBR, and Philophobia. Work is developed through contemporary production toolchains, with documented use of Logic Pro X and Pro Tools, and studio contexts involving SSL and Audient systems.

Her practice has been developed through documented studies, practice as research and residency periods across the world. Her international record includes UK-based study and additional residency and short-term work periods in the UK, France, Germany, and the US, documented across 2017-2025.
