Biography

Early years and emergence

Gurli Octavia began releasing music in the late-2010s, initially gaining attention through live performances and early demo releases on SoundCloud, where bedroom recordings reached thousands of streams. Between 2017 and 2019, she released three conceptually linked EPs — Philophobia, Lunacy (LACOBR), and Metanoia (Sony) — establishing a distinctive voice within the Danish music scene. The releases were accompanied by extensive live activity and growing radio support.

EP trilogy and live activity (2017–2019)

From 2017 to 2019 she released a three-part EP sequence that formed a coherent solo catalogue. The period included ongoing concert activity, showcases, and festival appearances across Denmark.

Debut album (2021)

In 2021 she released her debut full-length album I Could Be Blossoming Instead, expanding her scope in both production and format and anchoring a new phase of independent releases and performance work.
